Reporting & Analytics

Real-time dashboards, GST-ready reports, and deep analytics — all exportable to CSV, PDF, or directly to the GSTN portal.

Live Dashboard

Today's tokens, revenue, tonnage, outstanding dues, and top customers — all at a glance. Auto-refreshing widgets.

Sales & Purchase Register

Filter by date, party, or product. Full GST breakdown (CGST, SGST, IGST) with one-click CSV export for your accountant.

GSTR-1 & GSTR-3B

Auto-generated B2B, B2C, and HSN summaries. Export in GSTN portal JSON format — upload directly, zero manual work.

Weight Register

Token-wise weighment log with gross, tare, and net weight. Date range filters, vehicle type breakdown, and CSV export.

Profit & Loss

Monthly revenue vs COGS, gross profit, and margin percentages. Track profitability across financial years.

Stock Summary

Product-wise quantities purchased, sold, and closing stock. Closing value per product with full CSV export.

Outstanding & Ageing

Party-wise outstanding with ageing buckets: Current, 1-30, 31-60, 61-90, and 90+ days. Chase overdue payments instantly.

Inventory Analytics

Consumption trends, category breakdowns, top consumed items, and stock alerts. Daily/weekly/monthly granularity with charts.
